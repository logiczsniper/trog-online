Title: TROG: Adventures in the Stone Age

Release year: 1989

Author: New Concepts Software

About the game

Meet Trog, the missing link that everyone wishes was missing. He's been missing out all his life. To make matters worse, now his beautiful bride Rocksy is missing! The only thing that isn't missing is the fun as you play TROG: Adventures in the Stone Age.

Explore lost lands, hidden passages, ancient artifacts and rescue a beautiful woman. What could possibly be better on a Saturday night?!? Your skills as a master sleuth and adventure game player might, with a little perspiration and a lot of luck, get Trog past the many dangers that lie ahead in this world of pre-historic pandemonium!

Friend of foe? Only you can find out! You will lead Trog through a variety of colorful and imaginative scenes. The humuorous story line will leave you in stitches (and Trog in a body bag).
